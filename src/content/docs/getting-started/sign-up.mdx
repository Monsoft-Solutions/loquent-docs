## Go to the sign-up page

Open [app.loquent.io](https://app.loquent.io) in your browser. If you see the login screen, click **Create one** at the bottom of the form to reach the sign-up page.

<img src={signUpForm.src} alt="Loquent sign-up form showing fields for Your Name, Email, Password, and Organization Name with a Create account button on a dark-themed interface" />

## Create your account

Fill in the four required fields on the sign-up form:

| Field                 | Description                                      | Example           |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | ----------------- |
| **Your Name**         | Your full name                                   | `John Doe`        |
| **Email**             | The email address you use to sign in             | `you@example.com` |
| **Password**          | A secure password (minimum 8 characters)         | —                 |
| **Organization Name** | The name of your company or team                 | `Acme Inc.`       |

Click **Create account** to register. Loquent signs you in and redirects you to the dashboard.

## Explore the dashboard

After you sign in, the dashboard gives you an overview of your organization's activity.

<img src={signUpDashboard.src} alt="Loquent dashboard showing stat cards for Total Calls, Avg Duration, Pending Todos, and Active Contacts, a Call Activity bar chart, a Call Heatmap, and Quick Actions including Create Agent, Buy Phone, and New Contact" />

The dashboard includes:

- **Stat cards** — Total Calls, Avg Duration, Pending Todos, and Active Contacts at a glance.
- **Call Activity** — A bar chart showing call volume over the last 7 days.
- **Call Heatmap** — A grid displaying call distribution by day and hour.
- **Quick Actions** — Shortcuts to **Create Agent**, **Buy Phone**, and **New Contact**.

The sidebar on the left provides navigation to every section of the platform: Dashboard, Calls, Contacts, Phones, Agents, Knowledge, Analyzers, Todo Types, and Todos.

:::tip
Your account is ready. Head to [Create an Agent](/getting-started/create-agent/) to build your first AI-powered voice agent.
:::
