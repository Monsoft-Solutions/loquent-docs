Click the home icon in the sidebar to open the Dashboard. It gives you a real-time overview of your organization's call activity, open to-dos, and quick access to common actions.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/dashboard/dashboard.png" alt="Dashboard page showing Total Calls, Avg Duration, Pending Todos, and Active Contacts metric cards at the top, a Call Activity bar chart below, and a Call Heatmap" />

## Metric cards

The four cards at the top summarize activity for the selected date range:

| Card | Shows |
|------|-------|
| **Total Calls** | Number of calls received |
| **Avg Duration** | Average call length (mm:ss) |
| **Pending Todos** | To-dos that need review |
| **Active Contacts** | Contacts with recorded activity |

## Call Activity

The bar chart shows call volume by day for the selected period. Hover over a bar to see the exact count for that day.

## Call Heatmap

The heatmap shows when calls occur by day of the week and hour of the day. Darker cells indicate higher call volume, making it easy to spot your busiest times.

## Recent Calls and Todo Status

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/dashboard/dashboard-bottom.png" alt="Dashboard bottom section showing Recent Calls list with caller names and timestamps on the left, Todo Status with a Pending Review count on the right, and Quick Actions cards for Create Agent, Buy Phone, and New Contact" />

**Recent Calls** lists your five most recent inbound calls with the caller name, number, and timestamp. Click any row to open the full [Call Details](/calls/call-details) page.

**Todo Status** shows a breakdown of your open to-dos by status. Click through to [Manage To-Dos](/todos/manage-todos) to review and resolve them.

## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions section provides shortcuts to the most common setup tasks: creating an agent, buying a phone number, and adding a contact.

## Filter by date

All metrics and charts update based on the selected date range. Click the date selector in the top right to change the period.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/dashboard/dashboard-date-picker.png" alt="Date range picker open showing preset options: Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days (with a checkmark), Last 30 Days, Last 90 Days, All Time, and a Custom Range section with start and end date inputs" />

| Preset | Shows data from |
|--------|----------------|
| **Today** | Midnight to now |
| **Yesterday** | The previous full day |
| **Last 7 Days** | The past 7 days including today |
| **Last 30 Days** | The past 30 days including today |
| **Last 90 Days** | The past 90 days including today |
| **All Time** | Every call since your account was created |

To define a custom period, enter a start and end date in the **Custom Range** section. The filter applies to the dashboard metrics and the [Call History](/calls/call-history) list simultaneously.
