title: Contact Details

Click any contact card on the Contacts page to open their details. The Contact Details page shows the contact's name, all linked phone numbers, and all linked email addresses.

## Edit a contact's name

Update the **First Name** or **Last Name** fields and click **Save**. Changes take effect immediately.

## Phone numbers

The **Phone Numbers** section lists all phone numbers linked to this contact. Loquent uses these numbers to recognize the contact when they call. Click **+ Add Phone** to add a new number.

## Email addresses

The **Email Addresses** section lists all email addresses linked to this contact. Loquent uses the preferred email when sending automated emails through to-do actions. Click **+ Add Email** to add a new address.

## Delete a contact

Click **Delete** to permanently remove the contact. This cannot be undone. Phone numbers and emails linked to this contact are also removed.
