---
title: Add an Email Address
description: Link an email address to a contact so Loquent can send automated follow-up emails
---

Adding an email address to a contact enables automated to-do actions like **Send Email to Caller**. When Loquent executes a follow-up email task, it uses the contact's preferred email address as the recipient.

## Add an email address

Open a contact's details page and click **+ Add Email**.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/contacts/contact-add-email.png" alt="Add Email form appearing below the Email Addresses section heading with a field for the email address" />

Enter the email address and click **Add** to save it.

## Preferred email address

A contact can have multiple email addresses. Loquent always sends automated emails to the **preferred** address. If no address is marked as preferred, Loquent uses the first one on file.

:::caution
If a contact has no email address, to-do actions that send emails to the caller will fail. Add at least one email address to enable automated follow-ups.
:::

## Remove an email address

On the Contact Details page, expand the email address row and click **Remove**.
