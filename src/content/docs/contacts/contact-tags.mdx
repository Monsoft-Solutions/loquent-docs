Tags are color-coded labels you create for your organization and assign to any contact. Use them to segment your contacts, filter the Contacts list, and help the AI categorize callers automatically.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/contacts/contact-tags.png" alt="Contact Details left sidebar showing three color-coded tags — New Lead, Follow-up Needed, and Appointment Scheduled — below the contact name with an Add Tag button" />

## Create a tag

Tags are managed at the organization level. To create a new tag:

1. Go to **Settings → General** (or the Tags section if available).
2. Enter a tag name and choose a color.
3. Click **Save**. The tag is now available to assign to any contact.

## Assign a tag to a contact

Open a contact's [details page](/contacts/contact-details). In the left sidebar, click **+ Add Tag** and select a tag from the list.

## Remove a tag from a contact

Click the **×** next to the tag in the left sidebar to remove it.

## Filter contacts by tag

On the Contacts list, click **Tags** and select a tag to show only contacts with that label.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/contacts/contact-tags-filter.png" alt="Contacts list with the Tags dropdown open showing color-coded tags — New Lead is checked and the list shows one matching contact" />

## AI auto-tagging

After each call, Loquent analyzes the conversation and automatically applies relevant tags to the contact. A note labeled **AI Agent** appears in the timeline listing which tags were applied.

:::tip
Review AI tag suggestions after the first few calls to refine your tag library. The AI improves as it learns your organization's patterns.
:::
