## Open Contacts

Click **Contacts** in the sidebar. The Contacts page lists all contacts in your organization.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/contacts/contacts-list.png" alt="Contacts page showing a grid of contact cards with names like Maria, John Doe, and Jane Doe, each showing phone and email count badges" />

## Create a contact

Click **+ Create Contact** to open the contact form.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/contacts/contacts-create.png" alt="New Contact form with First Name and Last Name text fields and a Save button" />

Fill in the contact fields:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **First Name** | Yes | The contact's first name |
| **Last Name** | Yes | The contact's last name |

Click **Save** to create the contact. Loquent redirects you to the Contacts list where your new contact appears.

## How contacts work with calls

When a call comes in, Loquent matches the caller's phone number against your contact directory. If a match is found, the agent has access to the contact's name and history during the call. This enables more personalized conversations and helps analyzers generate richer to-do items.

:::tip
Add contacts for your frequent callers so agents can greet them by name and reference past interactions.
:::
