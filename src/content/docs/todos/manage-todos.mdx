---
title: Manage To-Dos
description: Review, approve, and reject tasks that Loquent extracts from your phone calls
---

The **To-Dos** page shows every task Loquent's AI extracted from your calls. Each card displays the task type, a description of the action to take, and its current status. Tasks that require your review have an **Approve** and **Reject** button.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/todos/todos-list.png" alt="The To-Dos page showing a pending review card for a Follow Up Email task with Approve and Reject buttons" />

## Approve a to-do

Click **Approve** on any card with status **Pending Review**. Loquent executes the task automatically — no further action needed. The status updates to **In Progress**, then **Completed** once done.

## Reject a to-do

Click **Reject** to dismiss the task. The to-do is marked **Rejected** and no action is taken. Use this when the AI extracted a task you don't want to act on.

## To-do statuses

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Pending Review** | Waiting for your approval |
| **Approved** | Queued for execution |
| **In Progress** | Being executed right now |
| **Completed** | Task finished successfully |
| **Failed** | Execution failed — check the card for details |
| **Rejected** | Dismissed by you |

:::tip
To skip the review step entirely, enable **Auto-approve** when creating a to-do type. Loquent will execute those tasks immediately after every call without waiting for your input.
:::
