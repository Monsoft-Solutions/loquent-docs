Agent instructions tell your agent how to behave — what to say, what to avoid, and how to handle different caller situations. Open an agent and click the **Instructions** tab to access the Instruction Builder.

## Generate instructions with AI

If you're starting from scratch, describe your agent in plain language and click **Generate Instructions**. Loquent's AI writes a complete set of instructions based on your description.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/agents/instruction-builder-generate.png" alt="Agent Instructions section showing a text area prompting the user to describe their agent in plain language and a Generate Instructions button below it" />

## Edit with the toolbar

Once you have instructions, use the toolbar to refine them with one click:

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Improve** | Rewrites for clarity, structure, and effectiveness |
| **Make concise** | Trims wordiness while keeping key instructions |
| **Improve tone** | Adjusts the agent's voice for better caller experience |
| **Add examples** | Inserts example conversations for common scenarios |
| **Fix issues** | Identifies and resolves ambiguous or conflicting instructions |
| **Strengthen safety** | Adds guardrails for sensitive situations |
| **Add personality** | Adds warmth, tone, and conversational style |
| **Enhance with org data** | Weaves in your organization profile details |

## Custom edit

Click **Custom edit** and type a free-form instruction to guide the AI with your own direction:

- "Make it more formal"
- "Remove the upsell section"
- "Add instructions for handling Spanish-speaking callers"

## Compare with original

After any AI edit, click **Show changes** to reveal the **Current** and **Original** tabs. Switch between them to compare the updated instructions against the previous version. Click **Revert** in the toolbar to undo the last AI edit.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/agents/instruction-builder-diff.png" alt="Instruction Builder with Show changes active, showing Current and Original tabs above the instructions pane and the AI toolbar with a Revert button at the bottom" />

## Enhance with org data

Click **Enhance with org data** to weave your [organization profile](/agents/organization-profile) — business name, hours, services, and location — directly into the instructions. This requires your organization profile to be filled out first.
