---
title: Call History
description: View a log of all calls handled by your agents, with recordings, transcriptions, and analysis
---

Click **Calls** in the sidebar. The Call History page displays every call your agents have handled, sorted by most recent first.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/calls/calls-list.png" alt="Call History page showing a table of calls with Date and Time, Phone Number columns, and a play button in the Recording column for each row" />

Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date & Time** | When the call occurred |
| **Phone Number** | The caller's number |
| **Recording** | Click ▷ to play the recording directly from the list |

Click any row to open the [Call Details](/calls/call-details) page with the full transcription and analysis.

:::tip
Call history populates automatically once your agent is assigned to a phone number and starts receiving calls. No setup is needed beyond the [Getting Started](/getting-started/welcome/) steps.
:::
