---
title: Send an SMS
description: Compose and send an outbound SMS message to a contact
---

Open a contact in the [Messaging Feed](/messaging/messaging-feed). Type your message in the compose bar at the bottom of the feed and press **Send** or hit Ctrl+Enter.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/messaging/send-sms.png" alt="Compose bar at the bottom of the messaging feed with a text input containing a message and an active Send button" />

## What happens after sending

The message appears in the feed immediately as an outbound message (right-aligned, amber background). Delivery status updates automatically via Twilio.
