The Organization Profile stores your business details in Loquent. Agents can reference this information during calls, and the [Instruction Builder](/agents/instruction-builder) uses it to generate accurate, context-aware instructions.

Go to **Settings → Organization** to fill out your profile.

## Profile sections

### Identity

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Description** | A short overview of what your business does |
| **Legal Name** | Your organization's name as agents should say it |
| **Industry** | Your business category (used to tailor agent tone) |
| **Services** | Comma-separated list of services you offer |
| **Founded Year** | The year your business was established |

### Contact & Web

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Public Phone** | Your main business phone number |
| **Public Email** | Your main business email address |
| **Website URL** | Your public website URL |
| **Appointment URL** | A booking or scheduling link |
| **Address** | Physical location agents can share with callers |
| **Service Area** | Geographic area your business serves |

### Languages

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Primary Language** | Default language for the organization (e.g. `en`) |
| **Timezone** | Used to interpret business hours correctly |
| **Secondary Languages** | Additional languages your agents can support |

### Operating Hours

Set your open hours for each day of the week. Toggle **Closed** for any day you're not open. Agents use this to tell callers whether your business is currently open and when to expect a callback.

### AI Context

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **After Hours Message** | What the agent should say when calling outside business hours |
| **Voicemail Message** | Message the agent leaves on voicemail |
| **Emergency Contact** | Emergency number or instructions for urgent situations |
| **Additional Context** | Any other background the agent should know |
| **Compliance Notes** | Industry-specific requirements the agent must follow |

Click **Save Profile** after making changes.

## Using org data in instructions

Once your profile is filled out, open the [Instruction Builder](/agents/instruction-builder) and click **Enhance with org data**. Loquent weaves your business name, hours, services, and location directly into your agent's instructions so it can answer questions about your business accurately.
