---
title: Settings
description: Configure your account, organization, telephony, integrations, and reporting
---

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to open the Settings page. Settings are organized into six tabs: **Account**, **General**, **Organization**, **Telephony**, **Integrations**, and **Reporting**.

## Account

The Account tab lets you update your login credentials.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/settings/settings-account.png" alt="Settings Account tab showing Change Email section with Current Email, New Email, and Current Password fields, and Change Password section with Current Password and New Password fields" />

### Change email

Enter your new email address and current password, then click **Update Email**.

### Change password

Enter your current password and a new password (minimum 8 characters), then click **Update Password**.

## General

The General tab controls your organization profile.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/settings/settings-general.png" alt="Settings General tab showing Organization Name field set to Loquent and a Save Settings button" />

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Organization Name** | The name displayed across the platform and in reports |

Click **Save Settings** after making changes.

## Organization

The Organization tab contains your full business profile — name, description, industry, contact details, languages, business hours, and AI context. Agents use this information during calls, and the Instruction Builder can pull it in to generate accurate instructions.

See [Organization Profile](/settings/organization-profile) for full details on all fields.

## Telephony

The Telephony tab configures your Twilio credentials for phone number management and call routing. By default, calls route through the system Twilio account. To use your own Twilio account, set up your credentials in this tab.

See [Bring Your Own Twilio](/settings/byo-twilio) for full setup instructions including credential validation, SMS event configuration, and phone number import.

### Phone number friendly names

Each phone number you own can have a human-readable label — for example, "Front Desk" or "Financing Department". These labels appear across the app to help you identify which number is which. To set a friendly name, open a number from the **Phone Numbers** page and edit the **Friendly Name** field.

## Integrations

The Integrations tab connects Loquent to your CRM or external system for automatic client lookups during calls.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/settings/settings-integrations.png" alt="Settings Integrations tab showing Client Lookup URL, Client Lookup Auth Token fields, Save Settings button, and a Test Integration section with a Phone Number field and Test Lookup button" />

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Client Lookup URL** | The API endpoint Loquent calls to look up caller information |
| **Client Lookup Auth Token** | The authentication token sent with lookup requests |

After saving your settings, use the **Test Integration** section to verify the connection. Enter a phone number and click **Test Lookup** to confirm the response.

## Reporting

The Reporting tab controls which reports are generated and who receives them.

<img src="https://wxaijzyufbt0o3u0.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/settings/settings-reporting.png" alt="Settings Reporting tab showing Daily reports and Per-call reports checkboxes both enabled, and a Report Email Recipients section with an email input field and Add button" />

| Setting | Default | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Daily reports** | Enabled | Receive a summary of all calls at the end of each day |
| **Per-call reports** | Enabled | Receive a report immediately after each call ends |

### Add report recipients

Enter an email address in the **Report Email Recipients** field and click **+ Add**. Reports are sent to all listed email addresses. Click **Save Settings** to apply changes.

:::caution
Telephony credentials and integration tokens are sensitive. Only share them with team members who need access to these settings.
:::
